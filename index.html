FAQ

Quick answers for lesson day.

Are range balls included?

No. Range balls must be purchased separately at the range.

What if I don't have clubs?

I can provide clubs.

How long is each lesson?

Each lesson is one hour.

Do you only teach at Bay View?

Yes, adult lessons are taught at Bay View. I also offer a junior class at 360 Ewa Beach Golf Course.

Do you offer short game lessons?

Yes. Short game lessons are available.

Where on the range should we meet?

I'm normally at stalls 1-2, but I'll text you about an hour before the lesson to confirm.

Do you offer video analysis?

Yes, video analysis is available.
